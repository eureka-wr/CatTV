# Cat meow audio sources

The promotional video uses real cat sound effects from Mixkit's free cat sound
effects library:

- `little-cat-attention-meow.wav`: Mixkit "Little cat attention meow" (SFX 86)
- `sweet-kitty-meow.wav`: Mixkit "Sweet kitty meow" (SFX 93)
- `domestic-cat-hungry-meow.wav`: Mixkit "Domestic cat hungry meow" (SFX 45)

Source page: https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/cat/

The Mixkit cat sound effects page describes the sounds as royalty free and ready
to use in projects under the Mixkit License.
